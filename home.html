Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the Unites States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States around 1905, brought by Mexican migrant workers who were hired for jobs in the mines and railroads in the Southwestern U.S. Initially considered a low-class street food, tacos were sold by women known as "Chili Queens". (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
